My Favorite Person

At first I was confused,
confused on what I love
or whom I love.

But as I saw a video
that make my heart flutter,
feel a butterfly in my stomach,
and make my smile go wider,
It is you.

There is no doubt in my mind
when I say you are my
favorite person.

There is nobody's smiles that
warms my heart like yours.
There is nobody's laugh that
i'd rather listen to. Nobody's
eyes I would rather get lost
in. There is nobody I would
rather spend hours on the
phone with until 3am.

You feel like home. I never
knew someone could make
me feel more comfortable
with just a touch or calm my
overthinking with just a glance
but you do. I am so grateful
to have you in my life.
I love you.
